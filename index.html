⚖️ Fixed vs Variable: The Core Tradeoff

What does "fixed" vs "variable" mean? Fixed revenue (service charge, apparel, vehicles) is guaranteed every week regardless of how many packages you deliver. Variable revenue (per-stop, per-package, e-commerce, fuel, LP mix) goes up when volumes are strong but goes down if FedEx sends you fewer packages. The question is: do you want a bigger guaranteed check (MESO 1) or bet on your volumes staying strong (MESO 3)?
